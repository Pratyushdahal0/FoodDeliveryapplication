Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get help with an order?

Add your order reference in the form and explain the issue clearly, such as late delivery, missing item, or wrong order.

Can restaurants contact FoodExpress?

Yes. Restaurant partners can use this page for registration, document verification, menu, order, and dashboard support.

Can riders ask for support?

Yes. Riders can contact support for signup, delivery issues, customer problems, payout issues, and technical errors.

How will I know my request was received?

After submitting the form, FoodExpress will show a reference number and send the request to the support team.
