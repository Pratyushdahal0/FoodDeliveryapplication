Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get help with an order?

Add your order reference in the form and explain the issue clearly, such as late delivery, missing item, or wrong order.

Can I report delivery issues?

Yes. Use the delivery issue option and include your order number so the support team can review the correct order.

How will I know my request was received?

After submitting the form, FoodExpress will show a reference number and save your request for admin review.

Can I ask about payments or refunds?

Yes. Payment and refund-related issues are reviewed carefully and can be escalated to admin support.
